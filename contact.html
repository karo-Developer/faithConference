We're located in the heart of Prayer City, easily accessible from major highways with plenty of parking available. Our campus includes the main sanctuary, fellowship hall, children's ministry building, and prayer garden.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect when I visit for the first time?

When you visit Faith Prophetic Prayer Network for the first time, you can expect a warm welcome from our greeting team, uplifting worship, relevant biblical teaching, and an opportunity to receive prayer. Our services typically last about 90 minutes. We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early to find parking and get settled. Be sure to stop by our welcome center after the service to receive a gift and meet some of our team.

What is available for children during services?

We offer age-appropriate children's ministry for infants through 5th grade during all Sunday morning services. Our children's ministry includes worship, Bible lessons, activities, and games in a safe, secure environment. All of our children's ministry volunteers are background-checked and trained. When you arrive, you can check your children in at our children's ministry kiosk where you'll receive a secure tag for pick-up after the service.

How can I get involved in ministry at the church?

We encourage everyone to get involved in ministry! The best way to start is by attending our "Ministry Connect" event held quarterly, where you can learn about all our ministry opportunities. You can also fill out a ministry interest form on our website or contact the church office. Many ministries have training sessions for new volunteers, and some require background checks (especially those working with children or vulnerable populations).

How do I submit a prayer request?

You can submit prayer requests in several ways: 1) Fill out the prayer request form on our website, 2) Call our 24/7 prayer hotline at (123) 456-PRAY, 3) Fill out a prayer request card during any service, or 4) Email prayer@faithprayernetwork.org. All prayer requests are confidential and shared only with our prayer team unless you specify otherwise. Our team prays over every request we receive.

Do you offer counseling services?

Yes, we offer biblical counseling for individuals, couples, and families. Our counseling ministry is led by trained counselors who provide guidance based on Scripture. To request counseling, please contact the church office to schedule an initial consultation. We also offer support groups for various life challenges including grief, addiction recovery, and divorce care. All counseling is confidential, and there may be a modest fee for extended sessions.
