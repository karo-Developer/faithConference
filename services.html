Our Services

Join us for worship, teaching, prayer, and fellowship as we grow together in faith

Regular Worship Services

We offer multiple services throughout the week to accommodate different schedules and provide opportunities for spiritual growth and community connection.

Sunday Morning Worship

Sundays at 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM

Our flagship services featuring powerful worship, prophetic ministry, and relevant teaching from God's Word. These services are designed to equip believers for the week ahead and welcome newcomers to our church family.

  • Dynamic praise and worship
  • Prophetic prayer ministry
  • Children's ministry available
  • Prayer teams available after service
Plan Your Visit

Wednesday Prayer & Teaching

Wednesdays at 7:00 PM

A midweek service focused on deepening your prayer life and understanding of Scripture. This intimate gathering includes teaching on prayer, prophetic ministry, and extended time for corporate prayer.

  • In-depth Bible teaching
  • Corporate prayer sessions
  • Prophetic ministry training
  • Small group discussions
Join Us This Wednesday

Friday Youth Night

Fridays at 7:30 PM

A high-energy service designed specifically for youth and young adults (ages 13-30). Featuring contemporary worship, relevant messages, and opportunities for connection and discipleship.

  • Contemporary worship band
  • Relevant messages for young people
  • Breakout groups by age
  • Games and fellowship time

Special Services

Sunrise Prayer

Join us every first Saturday of the month at 6:00 AM for a powerful sunrise prayer meeting.

Baptism Services

Quarterly baptism services for those ready to publicly declare their faith in Christ.

Communion

Monthly communion services on the first Sunday of each month during both morning services.

Healing Service

Monthly healing service with prayer for physical, emotional, and spiritual restoration.

Join Us Online

Can't join us in person? We livestream all our Sunday services and Wednesday prayer meetings. You can participate in worship, listen to messages, and even submit prayer requests online.

Our online community is growing with believers from around the world who connect with us each week. You can watch live or catch up later with our archived services.
