Our Ministries

Discover opportunities to serve, grow, and connect within our church community

Growing Together in Service

At Faith Prophetic Prayer Network, we believe every member has been gifted by God to serve others and build up the body of Christ. Our ministries provide opportunities for you to use your gifts, grow in your faith, and make a difference in our church and community.

Get Involved Today

Prayer Ministry

Prayer

The heartbeat of our church, this ministry is dedicated to intercessory prayer, prophetic prayer, and teaching believers how to develop a powerful prayer life.

Wednesdays 7 PM & Sundays after services
Open to all believers
24/7 Prayer Line: (123) 456-7891

Children's Ministry

Children & Youth

Faith Kids provides a safe, fun, and engaging environment where children ages 0-12 can learn about God through age-appropriate lessons, worship, and activities.

Sundays during both morning services
Ages 0-12, volunteers needed
Background-checked volunteers only

Youth Ministry

Children & Youth

Fusion Youth Ministry creates a space for teenagers (13-18) to encounter God, build authentic relationships, and grow in their faith through relevant teaching and activities.

Fridays 7:30 PM & Sundays 6 PM

Games, worship, small groups

Worship & Arts Ministry

Worship & Arts

Our worship team leads the congregation into God's presence through music, song, and creative arts. This ministry includes vocalists, musicians, dancers, and technical crew.

Rehearsals: Thursdays 7 PM

All skill levels welcome to train

Outreach Ministry

Outreach

This ministry serves our local community through food distribution, clothing drives, prison ministry, and evangelism outreaches to share God's love in practical ways.

Monthly outreaches, various times

Discipleship Ministry

Discipleship

Small groups and one-on-one mentoring to help believers grow in their relationship with God, understand the Bible, and apply biblical principles to daily life.

Groups meet throughout the week
Various group types available
Meet in homes or at church

How to Get Involved

Ready to serve and grow through one of our ministries? Here's how you can get started:

  • Pray about it - Ask God to guide you to the right ministry where you can use your gifts.
  • Get information - Attend our "Ministry Connect" event held quarterly to learn about all our ministries.
  • Meet with a leader - Connect with the ministry leader to discuss expectations and commitment.
  • Complete training - All ministry volunteers complete basic training and background checks where applicable.
  • Start serving - Begin your ministry journey with the support of our church family!
